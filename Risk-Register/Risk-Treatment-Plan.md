## Risk Treatment Plan 

This section outlines how each identified risk will be handled using standard treatment options: Mitigate, Transfer, Accept or Avoid.

| Risk ID | Treatment Option | Explaination | Owner | Due Date |
| R-001 | Mitigate | Strengthen access controls, enforce MFA, and conduct access reviews to reduce likelihood of unauthorised access | Security team | Q2 2026|
| R-002 | Mitigate | Implement phishing training, email filtering, and fraud monitoring to reduce attack success | Cybersecurity & Fraud Team | Q1 2026|
|R-003  | Mitigate | Improve system redundancy and disaster recovery capabilities to reduce downtime | IT Infrastructure | Q3 2026|
|R-004  | Mitigate | Conduct regular Compliance audits and update policies to ensure FCA alignment | Compliance Department | Ongoing |
|R-005  | Transfer & Mitigate | Use vendor contracts to transfer some liability while performing continous vendor monitoring | Vendor Management | Q2 2026 |
|R-006  | Mitigate | Apply least privilege, monitoring, and DLP tools to reduce insider misuse | Security Operations | Q1 2026 | 
|R-007  | Mitigate | Deploy EDR, backups, segmentation and IR playbooks to reduce ransomware impact | Cybersecurity Team | Q2 2026 |
|R-008  | Mitigate |Enforce password policy, MFA and training to reduce credential compromise | IT & HR | Q1 2026 |
